Prioritized Experiments

A strategic playbook to address the core bottleneck through validated learning.

Experiment 1: The Unified "Quant-Based" Demand Model

Hypothesis: We hypothesize that by consolidating our demand strategy and budget around a unified "Quant-Based" model, we will identify and double down on the most profitable Model-Channel Fit, thereby improving our LTV:CAC ratio and overall efficiency post-acquisition.

Test: Centralize reporting to analyze LTV:CAC and payback period across different customer segments and product lines (Sendoso, Alyce, Postal). Reallocate 20% of the paid media budget to the channels with the most favorable ratios for a 90-day period.

Bottleneck Addressed: Model-Channel Fit. This experiment will directly reveal which channels are truly profitable at scale, providing a data-driven path to more efficient growth.

Experiment 2: The QBG Acceleration Loop

Hypothesis: We hypothesize that by shifting from a purely top-of-funnel MQL focus to "Qualified Buying Group" (QBG) engagement during the active sales cycle, we will accelerate deal velocity and increase Closed-Won rates more efficiently.

Test: Identify 50 high-value target accounts in the "Evaluation" stage. Use Demand Gen resources (Ads, Content, Gifting) to simultaneously launch an "acceleration play" targeting every member of the QBG with personalized, value-based messaging.

Bottleneck Addressed: Model-Channel Fit. A faster sales cycle directly improves the efficiency of sales and marketing channels, making each dollar spent more impactful.

Experiment 3: The "Digital Unboxing" Conversion Lift

Hypothesis: We hypothesize that by creating a "Digital Unboxing Experience" on our website, we will more effectively convey the emotional value of Sendoso, thereby increasing address confirmation rates and improving our MQL-to-SQL conversion velocity.

Test: Apply Laja's diagnostic methodology (user tests, heuristic analysis) to the core conversion flow. A/B test a high-intent landing page with a video or interactive visual that simulates the physical unboxing experience, replacing a static form.

Bottleneck Addressed: Model-Channel Fit. A higher conversion rate on the website directly improves the efficiency of all channels driving traffic to the site.

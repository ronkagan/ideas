Prioritized Experiments

A hypothesis-driven plan to address the bottleneck and unlock transformational enterprise growth.

1: Focus Content on our buyer's buyer to gain enterprise credibility

Hypothesis:

Show Sendoso in the room with our buyer's buyer. Show that we are arbiters of the taste and the class needed to connect with the most sought after prospects and give them the megaphone of our platform to discuss the challenges of their niche industries in which our ABM marketing customers are focused.

Test:

B2B buyers want to hear from their prospects and peers instead of vendors or brands. Sendoso's most-qualified target buyers are already familiar with ABM and we know they are searching for swag. Why? To more meaningfully connect with their buyers.

So, let's find out what makes the top B2B prospects' hearts tick with excitement. Poduce pulitzer-prize worthy interviews with them. Reach out to key titans of industries where our customers want to connect more deeply with their prospects. Conduct vibrant roundtables with them and other luminaries. Place the concept of gifting and tactile experiences at the heart of the discussion. Turn the event into a series of regularly recurring, agenda-driven summits that celebrate the business of human connection. Make select contents of those events including the preferences of the most coveted B2B prospects in the world available to our customers.

Bottleneck Addressed:

Market-Brand Fit

2: Our prospects have questions. Let "Sendoso SensAI" answer directly in LLMs and beyond with MCP/API

Hypothesis:

A public-facing chatbot, "Sendoso Sensai" that capitalizes on our enterprise knowledge can be positioned as a powerful recommendation engine even to prospects without giving the whole of SmartSuite away. Winning By Design has a great example of a chatbot trained on their revenue architecture book, which can help generate deeply engaged leads.

Test:

Produce a chatbot that can be integrated into Claude or other LLMs via MCP or other applications via API. Wherever our "Sendoso SensAI" can help answer our prospects' questions, we are gaining the traction needed to hike up the upmarket mountain.

Bottleneck Addressed:

Market-Brand Fit

3: Recruit and train "Gift Geniuses" to lead "phygital" pop-up store and booth activations

Hypothesis:

For a physical product with a substantially digital dimension, it is critical to have our enterprise prospects get their hands on our physical products in a physical space that we have branded much the same way that retail customers get their hands on Apple products in an Apple-branded space to justify making a larger financial commitment than a non-Apple purchase.

Test:

Ultimately, for enterprise buyers considering dimensional marketing, we don't want them to simply send it, we want them to be convinced of the value of Sendoso-ing it. That comes from making a plan with a genius that can be a digital takeaway from the in-store consultation. They will leave not necessarily having purchased but equipped with everything they need to sell Sendoso internally with us at their organization.

Bottleneck Addressed:

Prioritized Experiments

A hypothesis-driven plan to address the bottleneck and unlock growth.

Priority 1: Strategic Content Shift

Hypothesis:

By shifting from tactical, item-focused content to strategic, revenue-focused "ABM Pipeline Playbooks," we will attract a higher-intent audience searching for solutions, not just products.

Test:

Launch a new pillar content piece and associated webinar titled "The ABM Pipeline Acceleration Playbook," promoted via LinkedIn and targeted content syndication, measuring MQL-to-SQL conversion rate against existing content benchmarks.

Bottleneck Addressed:

Market-Brand Fit

Priority 2: Website Messaging Refinement

Hypothesis:

Reframing the homepage value proposition from "The Sending Platform" to "The Revenue Engine for ABM" will increase demo requests from visitors with strategic, high-value titles.

Test:

A/B test the current homepage headline and sub-headline against new, ROI-focused messaging. Analyze the title and company size of demo requests from each variant over a 30-day period.

Bottleneck Addressed:

Market-Brand Fit

Priority 3: Channel & Influence Expansion

Hypothesis:

By building influence on channels where our target audience already pays attention (e.g., strategic ABM communities on LinkedIn), we can generate higher-quality demand than by competing on expensive, tactical paid search terms.

Test:

Launch a thought-leadership video series on LinkedIn featuring interviews with ABM leaders. Track engagement rates and direct traffic to a dedicated landing page, comparing the cost-per-qualified-lead to existing paid search campaigns.

Bottleneck Addressed:

Market-Channel Fit

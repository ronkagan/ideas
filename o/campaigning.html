Executive Summary: Solving the Revenue Bottleneck

The company's primary growth bottleneck was a hypothesized Signal-Metric Fit discrepancy.

The go-to-market relied on a lead-based (MQL) model, which is bad for complex, multi-stakeholder enterprise sales. This created significant friction between Marketing and Sales. Individual content downloads are poor indicators of an account's true buying intent.

This campaign shifted from a volume-based MQL model to a value-based Marketing Qualified Account (MQA) model. By focusing on account-level engagement signals across the entire buying committee, we doubled the effectiveness of the Marketing-Sales partnership and captured $1M in closed-won ARR from one of the largest banks in North America.

Prioritized Experiments: A Learning-Oriented Approach

1. Implement a Marketing Qualified Account (MQA) Model

Hypothesis

Shifting from MQLs to MQAs, based on a weighted account engagement score, will increase pipeline velocity by moving from outdated lead qualification SLAs to focus on buying committee behavior.

Plan

Define the MQA threshold based on multiple contacts from a target account engaging with high-value content. Run a pilot comparing the MQA-to-Opportunity conversion rate against the historical MQL-to-Opp rate.

Learn

Although titles were engaging that would ordinarily have been scored out, the MQA engagement threshold based on multiple contacts from a target account proved superior to an overly strict title-based qualification approach doubling the amount of meaningful opportunities created over the historical MQL-to-Opp rate.

Bottleneck Addressed

Signal-Metric Fit

2. Launch C-Suite Executive Content

Hypothesis

Fostering bespoke, C-level engagements among a peer group of CROs (on proving ROI, responding to regulatory risk) will secure more executive Sales interest than our current tactical, user-focused content.

Plan

Turn our content writer into a journalist interviewing CROs at Tier 1 accounts. Track meeting acceptance rate at the account before and after the article published.

Learn

Sales received 2x accepted meetings. after publishing the interview with the target company's CRO.

Bottleneck Addressed

Content-ICP Fit

3. Restructure Paid Channel Investment

Hypothesis

High-quality LinkedIn awareness leads can be targeted efficiently on Facebook for consideration stage content, accelerating pipeline velocity.

Plan

Reallocate budget. Use lower-cost programmatic display for "air cover" to the TAL. Use LinkedIn ads only to reach high-quality net-new names to engage from MQAs with awareness/MOFU-stage assets. Measure velocity of funnel progress for leads only targeted on LinkedIn vs. those targeted on LinkedIn and Facebook.

Learn

Leads that downloaded assets on LinkedIn and on Facebook were 2x as likely to accept meetings from Sales. This scaled programmatic display for "air cover" to the TAL while retaining the quality of LinkedIn lead filters.

Bottleneck Addressed

Model-Channel Fit

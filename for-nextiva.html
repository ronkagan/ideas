The Transformation for the Director of Marketing Operations

From Technical Debt to a Unified Growth Engine

Analysis reveals Nextiva's core operational challenge: a fragmented MarTech stack, with HubSpot and Pardot creating data silos that feed into Salesforce. This makes reliable attribution and ROI analysis nearly impossible. The transformation for a new Director is not to maintain the status quo, but to be a **transformer**: to architect and build a single source of truth that powers a predictable, data-driven growth engine and delivers the clarity leadership requires.

Diagnose & Align

Conduct a full audit of the tech stack and lead-to-revenue process to build a data-backed case for change.

Consolidate & Build

Lead the migration to a single marketing automation platform, creating a unified data foundation for the first time.

Measure & Innovate

Deploy a true multi-touch attribution model and activate audience intelligence to drive hyper-targeted campaigns.

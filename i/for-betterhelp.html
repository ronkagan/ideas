The Audience We Will Serve

Personas for Every Stage of Life

Our Lifecycle Marketing efforts will be focused on reaching individuals at key life stages where they are most likely to seek support. By understanding their unique challenges and online behaviors, we can create a supportive and effective journey to wellness.

The Young Professional

Navigating career stress, burnout, and work-life balance. They are looking for tools to manage anxiety and build resilience.

The New Parent

Dealing with postpartum challenges, relationship stress, and the identity shift of parenthood. They need flexible, accessible support.

The College Student

Facing academic pressure, social anxiety, and the transition to adulthood. They are seeking confidential, affordable mental health resources.

Where Our Audience Seeks Information

High affinity for trusted health and wellness sites like Psychology Today and Healthline confirms this persona's research-driven approach to finding care.
